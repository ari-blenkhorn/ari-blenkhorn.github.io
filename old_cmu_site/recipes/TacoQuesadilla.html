Taco Quesadilla

makes 1 quesadilla
Original source: Old El Paso recipe archive
One Taco Quesadilla contains 660 calories, 40 gm fat, 20 gm saturated fat, 115 mg cholesterol, 1230 mg sodium, 41 gm carbohydrate, 3 gm fiber and 34 gm protein.
Ari Rapkin

1 10" tortilla
2 oz. pepper jack cheese, shredded
1 oz. chopped green chiles
1/4 cup tomato, diced
1/4 cup taco filling, heated
1/8 cup shredded lettuce
1/8 cup sour cream
1/4 cup salsa

 Place the tortilla on a clean, dry grill. Sprinkle the cheese, chopped chiles and tomato over the entire tortilla, then top with the hot taco filling.

Fold the tortilla in half and cook until the tortilla begins to brown. Turn over and cook until the cheese is completely melted and the tortilla begins to brown.

Remove from grill and cut into 4 uniform wedges. Garnish with the lettuce and sour cream. Serve with salsa.
