What does NP stand for?

Some non-computer-science friends recently asked what it meant for a problem to be NP-complete. The guesses were a lot more fun than the actual answer!

  • Not Possible

  • Not Practical

  • No Point

  • Not P

  • Nasty Problem

  • Next Publication (what you get when you work out a new reduction?)
